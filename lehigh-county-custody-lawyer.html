What controls

The written order controls until it is changed.

Informal arrangements may not replace the operative court order. Identify legal custody, physical custody, exchange terms, communication rules and any restrictions before deciding what relief to request.

School, medical care, childcare, transportation and each parent's availability should be organized around the child's routine.

Do not wait on a listed date

If a paper lists a hearing, response, appeal or license date, use that date as the starting point.
