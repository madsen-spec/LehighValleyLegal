FAQ

Questions real callers ask.

Do I need to hire a lawyer or can I use the public defender?

If you qualify for the public defender, that is a real option and many public defenders are capable lawyers. Private counsel makes sense when you want a lawyer you choose, can afford the fee and need focused help with the specific risks in front of you.

What should I have ready before I call?

If you have a notice, order, citation, letter or complaint, keep it nearby. Also identify the county or court, any scheduled date and the names of the people involved.

Can anyone promise a specific result?

No. Outcomes depend on facts, law, evidence, procedure and decisions made by courts, agencies, opposing parties or insurers.

Can I share confidential facts right away?

No. Use the first contact for scheduling and a short description. Detailed confidential facts should wait until conflict review is complete.
