What controls

The highest-risk issues come first.

A criminal charge, DUI, PFA order, custody dispute or firearms restriction can create immediate court, safety, license, employment or family consequences. Identify every related docket, order and notice.

Employment, unemployment, estate, land-use, civil and injury matters remain available as secondary practice paths when the facts fit.

Do not wait on a listed date

If a paper lists a hearing, response, appeal or license date, use that date as the starting point.
