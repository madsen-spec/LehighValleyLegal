Firearms and rights restoration

Separate the denial, the license and the disability.

A PICS denial, an LTCF denial and a Section 6105 disability are different problems. They need different records and different fixes.

James's view: A pardon and an expungement are not interchangeable for rights restoration. The conviction type and the disability source matter.

What controls the next step

Core statutes include 18 Pa.C.S.A. § 6105, 18 Pa.C.S.A. § 6106, 18 Pa.C.S.A. § 6111 and 18 U.S.C. § 922(g).

PICS work often starts with PSP forms SP4-113 and SP4-197. PFA firearm relinquishment can also involve 23 Pa.C.S.A. § 6108(a)(7).

Next: If three DUIs in five years, a PFA order, an old misdemeanor or a federal disability may be involved, get the records before applying again.
