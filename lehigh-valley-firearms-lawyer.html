Local preparation

The exact paper controls the first question.

A firearms issue may involve criminal charges, a PFA or other protection order, a PICS denial, license-to-carry revocation, restoration question or record issue. Each category can involve different procedures and deadlines.

Do not rely on a general description alone. Bring the charge, court order, agency letter, denial notice, revocation document, docket information and any related criminal or family-court documents.

What matters first

Questions

Common preparation questions.

What should I bring for a PICS issue?

Bring the denial or challenge documents, transaction information, dates, agency correspondence and any old court records that may be involved.

Can a PFA or criminal case affect firearms?

It can. The actual order, charge, disposition and current law must be reviewed before conclusions are drawn.

Can this page tell me if I am eligible to possess a firearm?

No. Eligibility depends on detailed facts, records and law. This page only helps organize documents.
