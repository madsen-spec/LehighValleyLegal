Firearms records

Firearms questions should not be answered from memory alone.

A firearms issue may involve criminal charges, a PFA or other protection order, a PICS denial, license-to-carry revocation, restoration question or record issue. Each category may involve different procedures and timing.

Do not rely on a general description alone. The charge, court order, agency letter, denial notice, revocation document, docket information and related criminal or family-court history may all change the answer.
