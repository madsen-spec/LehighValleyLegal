Privacy and website data

What this website collects and why.

Lehigh Valley Legal limits website collection to information needed to operate the site, respond to requested contact and understand whether the site is helping visitors.

Important

  • Do not submit documents or detailed confidential facts
  • A callback request does not create representation
  • Analytics remains off unless you choose to allow it

Callback requests

Information you choose to send.

The callback form asks for contact information, the safest way to respond, matter category, county or forum, the next known date, names of the people involved and a short summary. The form does not accept uploads.

The information is used to route the request, check for potential conflicts, assess matter fit and arrange a response. It is not used to create an attorney-client relationship, and representation begins only after acceptance and written engagement terms.

Do not include Social Security numbers, financial-account information, passwords, medical records, evidence files or detailed confidential facts.

Current staging status

The public review build does not have a live intake destination. If routing is unavailable, the form states that no request was recorded and directs the visitor to call.

Analytics choice

Optional measurement.

The production site is designed to use Google Analytics 4 only after a visitor chooses to allow analytics. Before consent, the site does not load the Google Analytics library.

When allowed, measurement is limited to page activity and privacy-limited events such as phone-link clicks and callback-form starts or completions. Names, phone numbers, email addresses, other-party names, dates and form summaries must not be sent to analytics.

You can change the analytics choice from the privacy control shown on the site.

Search Console

Google Search Console may be used to understand how pages appear in search results. It does not receive callback-form contents from this website.

Phone and technical data

Calls and website operation.

A Lehigh Valley Legal-only forwarding number may be used to identify calls generated by this website. The office phone system and CRM may retain call-routing and disposition information under their configured policies.

The hosting provider necessarily processes technical information such as IP address, request time, requested path, browser information and security signals to deliver and protect the site. Public analytics and logs must not contain callback-form contents.

Information may be retained as reasonably needed for intake, conflict review, security, recordkeeping and legal obligations. Final production retention settings must be confirmed with the connected CRM, phone and hosting systems.

Questions or choices

Use the office phone number for questions about a callback request or website data. Use the privacy control to allow or decline optional analytics.
