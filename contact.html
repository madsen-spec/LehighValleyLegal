Before you share details

Use the initial message to identify the issue, where it is pending and the people involved. Save detailed facts until the office confirms that the matter can be discussed.

Helpful first details

  • county, court, agency or board involved;
  • hearing, filing, appeal or response date if one exists;
  • names of opposing parties, witnesses, insurers or employers;
  • the kind of order, complaint, citation, notice, contract or letter involved.

For emergencies or immediate safety concerns, use emergency services. For court, agency, license or filing deadlines, do not wait until the last day to seek legal help.
