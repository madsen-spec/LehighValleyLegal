Before you share details

Use the first contact to identify the issue, the next date and the people involved. Save detailed facts until the office confirms that conflicts have been checked and the issue can be discussed.

Helpful information to have ready

  • county, court, agency or board involved;
  • next hearing, filing, appeal or response date;
  • names of opposing parties, witnesses, insurers or employers;
  • the newest order, complaint, citation, notice, contract or letter.

For emergencies or immediate safety concerns, use emergency services. For court, agency, license or filing deadlines, do not wait until the last day to seek legal help.
