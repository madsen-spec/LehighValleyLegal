Before assuming an agency deadline

Employment claims often turn on timing and forum.

Employment timing can depend on the specific act, employer, agency and procedural history.

PHRC and EEOC timing

Public agency guidance identifies short filing windows for many employment-discrimination matters. Do not assume an HR process, union process, or internal appeal protects outside agency timing.

Preserve agency notices

If a PHRC or EEOC questionnaire, charge, notice, answer, dismissal or right-to-sue letter exists, keep the full notice and any envelope, email or portal date.

Employment questions

Employment questions.

Should I wait for an internal HR process to finish?

Do not assume an internal process pauses outside filing windows. Preserve documents and check timing promptly.

What information matters most?

Policies, schedules, pay stubs, discipline, termination letters, accommodation requests, messages, agency notices and dated timelines can matter.

Does every unfair workplace decision create a legal claim?

No. Employment law depends on facts, documents, statutes, employer conduct and available forums.
