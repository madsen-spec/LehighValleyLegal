Employment discrimination

Name the protected category and the adverse action.

Discrimination law is not a general unfairness system. A useful review starts with the protected category, the adverse action and the proof tying them together.

James's view: A retaliation claim can be stronger than the original discrimination claim if the record shows protected activity followed by a real workplace consequence.

What controls the next step

Pennsylvania claims may involve the PHRA, 43 P.S. § 951 et seq.; federal companions can include Title VII, the ADA and the ADEA.

Pennsylvania employees often start with PHRC intake, and federal claims may involve the EEOC Philadelphia District Office.

Next: Bring the timeline, HR complaints, discipline, termination notice, accommodation records and names of comparators or witnesses.

Employment questions

Employment questions.

Should I wait for an internal HR process to finish?

Do not assume an internal process pauses outside filing windows. Preserve documents and check timing promptly.

What information matters most?

Policies, schedules, pay stubs, discipline, termination letters, accommodation requests, messages, agency notices and dated timelines can matter.

Does every unfair workplace decision create a legal claim?

No. Employment law depends on facts, documents, statutes, employer conduct and available forums.
