PHRC and EEOC timing

Public agency guidance identifies short filing windows for many employment-discrimination matters. Do not assume an HR process, union process, or internal appeal protects outside agency timing.

Preserve agency papers

If a PHRC or EEOC questionnaire, charge, notice, answer, dismissal, or right-to-sue paper exists, keep the full document and any envelope, email, or portal date.

Questions

Common preparation questions.

Should I wait for an internal HR process to finish?

Do not assume an internal process pauses outside filing windows. Preserve documents and check timing promptly.

What papers matter most?

Policies, schedules, pay stubs, discipline papers, termination letters, accommodation requests, messages, agency papers and dated timelines can matter.

Does every unfair workplace decision create a legal claim?

No. Employment law depends on facts, documents, statutes, employer conduct and available forums.
