Know which divorce path you are on.

Mutual consent under 23 Pa.C.S.A. § 3301(c), separation-based divorce under § 3301(d), and fault grounds under § 3301(a) and (b) are not the same case posture.

James's view: Do not sign property paperwork just to get the divorce moving if you do not understand the retirement, debt and real-estate consequences.

What controls the next step

Equitable distribution under 23 Pa.C.S.A. § 3502 is separate from APL, spousal support and alimony.

Northampton County divorce matters can involve pleadings, support court, discovery and a Master's hearing process.

Next: Bring tax returns, pay information, deeds, account statements, debt records and any support or custody orders.

Divorce questions

Divorce questions.

What should I talk about first?

Focus on what has changed: filing status, living arrangements, parenting time, support, access to money, property concerns and any scheduled court event.

Can divorce involve custody or support at the same time?

Yes. Divorce, custody, support, alimony and PFA issues can overlap, but each may have its own procedure and timing.

Does this page give legal advice?

No. It provides general information. Advice depends on the actual facts, filings and timing.
