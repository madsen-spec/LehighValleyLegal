What controls

The location of the case matters more than the mailing address.

An Allentown legal matter may be handled in a magisterial district court, the Lehigh County Court of Common Pleas, a state agency, PennDOT or a municipal office. The notice or docket should identify the forum.

The physical office for Lehigh Valley Legal is in Easton. Allentown and Lehigh County are service areas; no Allentown office is represented on this site.

Do not wait on a listed date

If a paper lists a hearing, response, appeal or license date, use that date as the starting point.
